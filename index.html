CFD Analysis on an undulated wing

This study employs Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) to investigate the flow behavior around a bio-inspired wing featuring humpback whale-like leading-edge undulations. The geometry was designed in SOLIDWORKS, and high-fidelity simulations were performed using the CRADLE software suite. A detailed convergence study with systematic mesh refinement validated the results, which include analysis of drag coefficients, pressure distribution, velocity vectors, and streamlines at various angles of attack. The project aims to elucidate the mechanisms by which biomimetic tubercles modify laminar and turbulent flow regimes to influence aerodynamic performance.

Finite Element Analysis of a Torque Arm

This project conducted a structural and fatigue analysis of a torque arm under combined static and cyclic loading using Abaqus FEA and MATLAB. I performed a mesh convergence study, applied von Mises and Gerber failure criteria, and optimized the design by reducing thickness by 17–25% while achieving a fatigue safety factor of 3,092. The work demonstrates expertise in simulation-driven design, failure analysis, and design for lightweighting.

Tuned Mass Dampeing System

This project involved designing, modeling, and validating a Pendulum Tuned Mass Damper (PTMD)—similar to the system in Taipei 101—to reduce vibrations in a high‑rise building caused by earthquakes and wind. Using MATLAB/Simulink and Simscape Multibody, I developed a high‑fidelity coupled simulation integrating a 3D spatial pendulum damper with a multi‑story building model. This end‑to‑end simulation project showcases strong skills in dynamic systems modeling, vibration control, multi‑physics simulation, and MATLAB/Simulink programming, with direct relevance to seismic engineering, wind engineering, and aerospace/automotive vibration mitigation.

3D Surface Modelling of a Car

Designed and prototyped a project to create a 1:10 scale model of a classic Ferrari 308. Utilized Autodesk Fusion 360 to construct the car's complex geometry through advanced surface modeling, using orthographic canvases as precision guides. Transferred the completed model to Ultimaker Cura for slicing and successfully 3D printed the final prototype on a Prusa i3. This project highlights skills in converting 2D references into manufacturable 3D models and executing an end-to-end digital fabrication workflow.
